About the work from home job/internship
As a Data Analytics intern, you will have the exciting opportunity to apply your Python, SQL, Data Analytics, MS-Excel, Data Science, Power BI, Tableau, Data Analysis, Data Cleaning, Data Manipulation, Data Preparation, Data Interpretation, and Data Extraction skills in a real-world business setting. Your role will involve working closely with our data team to extract, clean, manipulate, and interpret data to drive key business decisions.

Key responsibilities:
1. Use Python and SQL to extract and manipulate large datasets for analysis.
2. Utilize MS-Excel for data cleaning and preparation tasks.
3. Create visually appealing and interactive dashboards using Power BI and Tableau.
4. Conduct thorough data analysis to identify trends and insights for business improvement.
5. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and deliver data-driven solutions.
6. Assist in developing data models and algorithms to optimize business processes.
7. Present findings and recommendations to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner.

If you are passionate about data analytics and eager to gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced environment, this internship is an ideal opportunity for you to enhance your skills and make a valuable contribution to our team. Join us and be part of a dynamic and innovative team driving data-driven success!
